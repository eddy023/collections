The above figure shows the basic model of machine learning. We can express them using a simply math formula as:

$$ y = h(\sum_{i=0}^n \theta_i \times x_i) $$

$$x$$ and $$y$$ are the input and output of this model; $$h$$ represents "hypothesis". For any known machine learning problem, people follows this model trying to learn a good "hypothesis" or guess to help them predict the future.
