1. Probability & Likelyhood Functions

Probability functions (e.g., CDF, PDF) are used to represent the "chance" of a variale at any given "point" (notice the difference between x and dx, discrete and continuous), with a bunch of parameters featuring the functin. While for likelihood function, it is to estimate the parameters of feature with set of observed points, by maximizing the function.
